# AI Village Help Portal Specification

## Goal

Help rural citizens access important public services digitally.

## Features

- Government services
- Farmer crop support
- Health guidance
- Complaint reporting
- Scholarships
- Jobs and skills
- Emergency contacts
- AI help assistant

## Users

- Villagers
- Farmers
- Students
- Senior citizens
- Women
- Youth

## Future Scope

- Telugu voice support
- Real AI chatbot
- GPS complaint tracking
- Admin dashboard
